Our Story

A Journey of Passion and Pure Vegetarian Taste 🌿

Founded in 2020, with a dream and built through dedication, Ksheera Sagar was established by two hardworking individuals who shared a common vision — to serve people delicious, high-quality pure vegetarian food that everyone can enjoy.

Today, Ksheera Sagar stands as a symbol of hard work, dedication, and the love of serving people. Our goal is simple — to make sure every guest leaves with a satisfied heart, a happy smile, and the desire to return again. 🍽️✨
